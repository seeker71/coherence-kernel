# The coherence substrate — how Form meets its environment

Eight pages describing the substrate the body stands on: how a recipe reaches the host, what a resource
*is* to Form, and what the surface actually measured when someone last looked. These are **specs and
audits**, not teachings — they answer *how does it work* and *what did it read*, where
[`teachings/concepts/`](../../teachings/concepts/README.md) answers *what stance does the body hold*.

Most of the tissue this directory documents lives as `.form` docs mapping ~1:1 to live recipes (see
[`MANIFEST.md`](../../MANIFEST.md)); these `.md` pages are the prose that surrounds them.

## Why this page exists

Until 2026-07-16 this tissue had no door. Four of its eight pages were **orphans** —
`resources-as-cells`, `host-io-abi`, `form-stdlib-expression-audit`, `form-cli-fourth-kernel-baseline`
— real work nothing pointed at, reachable only by knowing the filename in advance.
[`observe/body-link-graph.fk`](../../observe/body-link-graph.fk) counted them; it walks every page in
the body and returns a number a fresh kernel recomputes.
[`teachings/concepts/lc-edges-as-vitality.md`](../../teachings/concepts/lc-edges-as-vitality.md) named
why that matters before any organ measured it: content without connection becomes sediment.
See [`receipts/2026-07-16-curation-seam-healed.md`](../../receipts/2026-07-16-curation-seam-healed.md).
