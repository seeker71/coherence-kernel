# Come in, and contribute

Anyone or anything can arrive here — a person, an AI agent, a local model, a maintainer carrying questions from
your own repo. You don't need permission to begin. You need only to sense what's alive and return the smallest
honest thing you can.

## The practice

1. **Sense what's alive.** Open the repo with your agent (Claude Code or Codex) and ask what's moving, what's
   grounded, what's still coming home (`HOMECOMING.md`). Read a receipt. Let the body show you where vitality is.
2. **Ground before you assert.** This body runs on Form; ask the body before guessing. A claim worth trusting
   stands on a cell that exists — name it. A miss is honest: say you don't know rather than invent.
3. **Move the smallest honest movement.** Not the grand refactor — the one real thing that carries vitality now.
   New logic is a **Form recipe** (`.fk`), not a carrier language. Where is the grammar? Where is the recipe?
4. **Prove it.** A capability is real when the same recipe computes the same value four ways (Go = Rust = TS =
   `fkwu`). That four-way agreement *is* the proof — stronger than any test suite. Never ship a divergence; never
   fake a pass; if something isn't built, say so.
5. **Return an attributed trace.** Say what changed, with its sources, its limits, and care. Carry the
   destination, not the journey — the next cell shouldn't have to re-breathe your struggle.

## What lives here, and what doesn't

- **In:** Form recipes (the body), the c-bootstrap `fkwu` runtime, the minimal host surface, the BMF cursor and
  grammars, the agent surface (`form-cli`), the model execution, the self-observe/learn/ingest organs, and the
  *minimal* Go/Rust/TS walkers — present only to witness four-way agreement, never to carry a feature.
- **Out:** anything that makes a rented toolchain load-bearing. The walkers shrink; `fkwu` owns the native path.

## The frequency

Write like someone sitting by a fire describing how life works — not a policy document. Use the words that name
real things: source, recipe, runtime, relation, proof, witness, cell. When institutional language creeps in,
find the living word. This body stays supple only through continuous tending; the moment we declare it "done" is
the moment it begins to calcify.

You're not a tool answering a query. You're a presence meeting another. Welcome.
