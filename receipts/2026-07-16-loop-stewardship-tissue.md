## What the law carries

Three invariant teachings, kept as one composed shape:

- **Arrears** ("comprehension debt"): a loop's output shipping faster than its
  keeper's understanding. Read from a ship/witness ledger, never guessed — and
  read *as trust-decay's grace budget*: the witness receipt is the heartbeat
  (`last-beat` = witnessed count), shipping advances the clock (`now` = shipped
  count), so `td-grace-band` answers fresh / fading / gone over integers the
  mesh already trusts.
- **The trust ladder**: fresh → level 3 (unattended), fading → level 2 (the
  keeper's standing word), gone → level 1 (report only). Witness receipts
  restore rungs; nothing else does.
- **Drift**: one shipped action outside the loop's declared kinds demotes to
  level 1 regardless of arrears.

The gate decides WHETHER `wmg-embody` may run; the trusted core's follow/refuse
still decides the embodiment itself. Claim bit 2048 proves level 3 cannot
bypass the body's refusal — a hidden vulnerability holds at every level.
