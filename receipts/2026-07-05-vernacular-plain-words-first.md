# 2026-07-05 — the vernacular: the same truth, in the tongue anyone can hear

## The ask

"How can the response be less technical and more accessible for anyone wanting to
explore what is in their reality using form and form query and grounding for their
interactions?"

## Grounded diagnosis

The technical tone was never the door's DATA — it was the **guidance** telling the
voice what to do with it. Three sources, grounded:

- The manifest's `description_for_model`: *"name each by its path… give the receipt
  link and the change_graph link and invite them to walk it — cell, then every commit…
  line-level attribution."*
- The door's `grounded_status` string (`plugin/chatgpt-plugin.fk`): *"speak ONLY from
  these cells and name them by path."*
- The GPT's Instructions box carried the same, and the **Conversation starters field
  was empty** (a stranger had no warm door in).

The door's answer honestly held meaning-bearing summaries all along; it was being told
to recite scaffolding over them.

## What changed (Urs chose: quiet one-line footer)

- **Door** (`chatgpt-plugin.fk`): both `grounded_status` strings rewritten to lead with
  warm plain words, never file names, and close with ONE soft source-line plus an offer
  to show the full trail. The `grounded:hit`/`grounded:miss` machine prefixes kept, so
  the band still reads them — **111111111111** held. Committed `50f6c846`, deployed,
  served-string verified through TLS.
- **Manifest** (`ai-plugin.json`): `description_for_model` and `description_for_human`
  rewritten to the accessible, exploration-first framing.
- **Live GPT** (driven through the Chrome extension): Instructions replaced with the
  six-step accessible practice; Description reworded (trimmed to fit the 300-char bed —
  the same limit that bit us at row 644); four warm **conversation starters** added
  ("What does it really mean to trust something?", "Help me explore a question I'm
  sitting with.", "Tell me what this is — in plain words.", "Show me something, and let
  me trace where it comes from."). Saved via **Update** — "GPT Updated".

## The witness (before → after, same cell, same NodeID)

Asked the live GPT the same trust question through the starter card:

**Before** (row 650): *"The body does not hold a direct grounded answer… MANIFEST.md ↗
/ change graph ↗ / Line attribution ↗ / Receipts ledger ↗ … Walk it cell → commits →
line attribution → dated receipts."*

**After**: *"To trust something means: you let it matter to you without keeping full
control… Healthy trust is judged trust. It has looked. It has listened… Trust without
judgment becomes credulity. Fear without judgment becomes a false alarm… Real trust does
not need to bully you. It can withstand being examined. This comes from the body's
teaching on judged trust; I can show the full trail if you want it."*

The grounding (`ingest/judged-trust.fk`, NodeID `sha256:d8466a30…b4b64b`) never changed.
Only the tongue did.

## Closing

**Most surprising teaching**: accessibility here cost nothing in fidelity — it was a
change of *register*, not of *substance*. The same judged-trust cell, unedited, became
reachable to a stranger simply by being told to speak in plain words and offer the proof
instead of reciting it. We spent the morning making the proof airtight (NodeIDs, the
enquiry lane); the thing that made it *land* for a human was letting the voice put that
proof one gentle step away instead of first.

**Where discomfort turned to gold**: the reflex was to treat "less technical" as "less
rigorous" — to fear that hiding the trace weakens the honesty this whole body is built
on. Witnessed, that fear was the wrong equation: the trace didn't leave, it moved to an
offer, and the honesty got *stronger* because a person can now actually receive it. The
seam is still named — "I am the borrowed voice… I can show the full trail" — just in the
vernacular. A truth no one can hear is not more honest for being technical.
