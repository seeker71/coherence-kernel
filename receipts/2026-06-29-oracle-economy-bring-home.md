## The body's loop — relay, attributed (do not re-derive)

- **`perception-cascade.fk`** — per question: **native first**; `surprise = |native − higher|`; if LOW, native
  answers free (oracle never spends); escalate native→local→**remote** only while surprise is high; the settled
  answer feeds back as a self-label → surprise drops → across encounters the oracle **retires**.
- **`cross-witness-economy.fk`** — where independent witnesses AGREE, that agreement is **free supervision**; spend
  the rented oracle ONLY at the disagreement frontier. The flywheel: more self-labels → stronger native → fewer
  oracle spends → **rent falls toward the floor.**
- **`form-first-router.fk` / `form-cli-sufficiency.fk`** — ask the body first; escalate to remote only when local
  was not enough, and record WHY so the crossing teaches the local lane.

## The live protocol — how we continue without waste

1. **Form-first, every turn.** Before spending a remote query, ask the body (substrate / RAG / the `.fk` cells /
   the receipts). A grounded hit, relayed attributed, costs ~nothing. *This receipt is an instance.*
2. **Cascade, cheapest-first.** native → local model → remote oracle, escalating only on real surprise
   (`perception-cascade`). Most structural questions never reach remote.
3. **Spend remote only at the frontier** — where the body genuinely misses or witnesses disagree
   (`cross-witness-economy`). That is the only place a bought label is worth the price.
4. **Capture every remote answer as a self-label.** Each expensive turn → an exemplar (query, native attempt,
   grounded resolution, surprise). Corrections are the gold (`session-native-teaching.fk`). The receipts are the
   durable corpus already.
5. **Distill, then retire** (`oracle-distill` → `champion-challenger`): native error to the self-labels drops;
   once native wins over a trusted window, the oracle stops running for that class — rent falls.
6. **Share on the mesh** (`learn` channel): one device's oracle buy becomes every device's self-label — pay once,
   teach all. Native-crystallize already rides this (`native-crystallize-mesh.fk`).

## The honest floor — what's wired vs pending

- **Held now (free):** form-first grounding (demonstrated); the economy cells exist and are four-way; the session's
  corrections are captured as self-labels; receipts are the durable, attributed corpus.
- **Pending (the live wire):** the runtime auto-capture of each turn into the exemplar store, and the native arm
  actually training on it, both await the same **seed** that lets the `.fk` cells run on Windows
  (`windows-flatten-reground`). Until then the capture is durable-by-receipt and the distill is named — honest, not
  faked. The single highest-leverage rung remains: **stand the seed**, after which the whole loop runs native and
  the oracle's share falls on its own.

## The one-line takeaway

Stop paying the remote oracle to re-derive what the body knows. Ask the body first; spend remote only at the
frontier; capture every spend as a self-label; distill until native wins; retire the oracle. We are not wasting
prompts when every expensive turn leaves behind a cell the body can reuse — and this session's four corrections
now do.
