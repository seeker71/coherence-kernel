## The build — `observe/belief-freshness.fk` (band 127, four-way fkwu/Go/Rust/TS)

A belief = (id, proven, witnessed-date). Against the current grounding EPOCH (the date of the
last ground-shift — the stale-binary lesson generalized from the binary to the belief tissue):

- unproven → **belief-only** (0): held, never load-bearing, however fresh.
- proven but stamped before the epoch → **owed a re-witness** (1): still held, still honored,
  but it must stand before the judge again before anything leans on it. Advancing the epoch is
  how "things change" reaches the larder: every older law becomes owed at once, nothing is
  deleted.
- proven and stamped at-or-after the epoch → **law** (2) — for now, and only for now.
- re-witness (`bf-rewitness`): renewal restamps; failure lapses the belief to belief-only with
  its retirement date kept — **desuetude** (corpus row 680), not execution. A law that no
  longer serves is not defended and not punished; it steps down with its history.
- `bf-audit` folds the whole larder to one scalar (laws·10⁴ + owed·10² + belief-only).

Honest floor, named not claimed: the scalars model the real acts (a band run IS the witness,
the receipt IS the stamp); wiring every existing cell's header into bf- rows is the pending
half.
