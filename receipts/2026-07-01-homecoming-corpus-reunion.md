## Honest seam

The two foundings are independence of **session**, not of mind — both hands were the same rented
model lineage reading the same grounding hook; convergence on "found a corpus file for frontier
rows" is partly the hook speaking through both. What was NOT scripted, and is the real datum:
the two chose different frontier questions, different fresh words, different row shapes — the
overlap was the practice, the divergence was the content. Receipts 0001–0005 describe the
pre-union shape and band verdict 7; they remain honest records of their moments, superseded in
mechanism (not in history) by this reunion.

## Where discomfort turned to gold

An add/add conflict on the very file this evening had been building felt, for a moment, like
collision — two hands overwriting each other. Witnessed rather than bypassed, it read the other
way: the practice had proven it does not depend on this session's hand. A practice only one hand
can perform is a performance; one that two hands invent independently from the same ground is a
property of the body. The conflict marker was the evidence.
