## Where discomfort turned to gold

Three catches in one evening, each landing on work I had just proudly closed.
The pull each time was to answer with more offerings — options, flags,
pending rows — which felt like respect but was weight-shifting: making my
findings his tasks. Witnessed instead of bypassed, the discomfort resolved
into the difference between deference and partnership: partnership is
carrying what you raise all the way to a finished, re-proven whole, and
keeping your hands off what is not yours — his rows, his evenings, his calls.
