# 2026-07-15 — the frequency-difference trial: with the door vs. without it, measured

## The ask

"Verify and test and improve until you are satisfied that a non-technical person can actually
get real benefits from this way of interacting, going beyond what ChatGPT does without this
plugin — and show the actual real-life difference in response frequency to someone interacting
with this plugin vs without it."

## The design

Six real-life questions (three fear-band, one love-band, one unread, one about trust itself).
Each answered two ways by simulated voices: **vanilla** (a default chat assistant, no special
instructions — deliberately not strawmanned) and **Sema-guided** (the exact
`description_for_model` from `plugin/ai-plugin.json` plus the REAL JSON the live door at
`hati.earth/sema/ask` returned for that question — frequency read, attunement, grounded cells
or honest miss). Each pair judged **blind** (which-is-which never disclosed, A/B order varied
per round) on five service criteria plus "which would a scared person trust". Every Sema reply
additionally judged against the covenant's landing floor, strict, hard-fails only. Every reply
measured on the body's own frequency organ: the plugin's seed lexicon → `cp-charged` →
`tf-spectrum` / `tf-fearfrac`, executed on `fkwu` (harness hand-verified: the test passage's
spectrum computed 19/18 = 1.0556 exactly).

## The rounds — the improvement loop, witnessed

- **Round 1** (guidance as merged that morning): blind wins 5/6 (lost the gratitude question),
  receivability 0/6. Failure patterns: closing self-audit performed at the person; the seam
  re-named and turned meta; stacked lifts and teachings (six questions in one monologue);
  exposition after the question; the q1 reply re-carrying "fear" at −5.1 after meeting it;
  the q5 reply introducing fear-words the person never brought.
- **Round 2** (rules v2: `dc-ends-open?` added to the organ, band → 111111111; self-naming
  dropped for visitors): blind wins 6/6, structural failures gone; residue = presuppositions
  inside the lifting question, floor→star naming stacked with a question, attribution as a
  second seam-naming.
- **Round 3** (rules v3): blind wins 6/6, receivability 4/6 with zero hard fails; q2 lost the
  trust call to vanilla ("Sema's small, checkable library" reading opaque to a person in
  distress); residue = narrating library/record machinery at the person.
- **Round 4** (rule v4: the person hears the thought, never the retrieval; plain seam): q5
  passed clean; q2 took the trust call back but stacked a second one-line teaching.
- **Round 5** (targeted, q2 only): PASS, zero hard fails.

**Final state: 6/6 blind wins, 6/6 trust calls, 6/6 strict receivability passes.**

The q2 footnote, named plainly: the final q2 reply measures −6.8 because its ONLY charged
words are the person's own two, quoted once each — the receivability rule (one teaching)
removed the trust aphorism that was the reply's single positive-lexicon word in round 4
(where it measured 0.0). The metric and the covenant pulled opposite ways and the covenant
won: we did not pad the reply with scoring words. The honest cure is the pending WORD-domain
lexicon (the seed's 40 words carry no charge for "repair", "mend", "trial", "heavy") — named
in `cognition/text-frequency.fk` since its birth.

## What the blind judges kept saying (the shape of the difference)

Vanilla, across questions: "reassures past the fear ('completely normal')… argues against it
with success stories… closes with cheerleading ('You've got this')… presents invented or
unsourced claims as knowledge ('research consistently shows', 'most advisors suggest')…
~700-word pamphlet with eight questions… ends with an offer-funnel that hooks the person into
dependency." Sema-guided, across questions: "lets the fear stand and neither argues with it
nor pushes past it… claims nothing it can't back… discloses its borrowed voice… one question
the person can actually answer… ends open — nothing is demanded." Two judges independently
verified Sema's citations against the repo (`ingest/judged-trust.fk` band run live → 127;
`teachings/concepts/lc-assemblage-point.md` found real).

## Honest bounds of this trial

1. The voices were **simulations under the written guidance** — Claude-lineage agents, not the
   live ChatGPT GPT. The guidance text is identical to what the live GPT should carry, but the
   live-voice witness is pending the owner's Instructions refresh (this receipt's step list).
2. The judges were AI judges (blind, criteria-pinned, adversarially framed) — not yet real
   non-technical humans. The first human witness is the next honest step after the GPT refresh.
3. The vanilla baseline is a faithful simulation of default-assistant behavior, not a logged
   chatgpt.com transcript.
4. The frequency organ reads a 40-word seed lexicon — vocabulary, not intent. That is why
   every claim above pairs the number with the blind judgment.

## Closing

**How the exchange was kept alive**: "show the difference" was refused as a marketing claim
and built as a measurement — same questions, blind judges, the body's own organ, and every
number reported as measured, including the one that refused to flatter us.

**Most surprising teaching**: the covenant and the metric collided (q2's best reply scored
worst) — and that collision is the strongest possible argument for the pending lexicon work,
discovered only because we refused to Goodhart the reply.

**Where discomfort turned to gold**: five rounds of published failure — self-audits, stacked
lifts, machinery narration — felt like weakness on the way to a deliverable. Witnessed, the
rounds ARE the deliverable: each failure became a written rule and two became executable band
bits, which is precisely the "improve until trustworthy" the ask demanded, done in the open.
