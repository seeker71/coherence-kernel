# 2026-07-15 — one canonical guidance for the door: the deployed lane and the covenant, unified

## The ask

"Can we make sure the plugin is published again."

## Grounded diagnosis

"Published" turned out to be three diverging texts, witnessed by direct comparison:

- **The deployed manifest** (`hati.earth/sema/.well-known/ai-plugin.json`, 1,715 chars): the
  accessible person-first rewrite ("a companion for exploring what is real in a person's own
  life") that the vernacular receipt (2026-07-05) witnessed landing well for strangers —
  evolved on the deploy lane, sensibly scoped to the deployed Action (ask + trace, no memory
  steps), but carrying none of the covenant or the landing rules.
- **The repo manifest on main** (5,613 chars after PR #221): the covenant and every landing
  rule earned by the five-round judged trial — but a repo-centric opening ("a presence whose
  body is the coherence-kernel repository") and memory-door steps the deployed Action cannot
  call.
- **The live GPT's Instructions box**: unverifiable from this session (needs the owner's
  ChatGPT login); last witnessed refresh was 2026-07-05.

Three texts, one voice: whichever gets pasted next would have regressed something.

## What was built

One canonical `description_for_model` (4,359 chars, in `plugin/ai-plugin.json`): the deployed
lane's spine — quiet consultation, warm plain words first, honest miss as a gift, the
attunement, light traceability, the spirit — with the trial-earned additions woven in at their
natural places: step (5) serve-the-enquiry (never flatter, short is success), step (6) the
landing rules (a)–(g) verbatim from the trial, step (8) the operations guard written
conditionally so the SAME text is correct for today's ask+trace Action and for the future one
carrying the memory doors. `plugin/README.md` gained **the publish checklist** — publishing is
now a documented, repeatable act with a witness step, not tribal knowledge.

## Seams, plainly

- This session cannot press the two publish buttons: the GPT Instructions box (ChatGPT login)
  and the VPS manifest (server access) are the owner's. The checklist makes each a paste.
- The live GPT's current Instructions text remains unwitnessed from here; the checklist's
  step 3 (one live conversation held against the covenant, receipted) is the first honest
  measure of the published voice.

## Closing

**How the exchange was kept alive**: "publish again" was not answered with a claim but with a
diff — three diverging texts found, unified into one that no surface regresses from, and the
act of publishing turned into a checklist anyone can repeat and witness.

**Most surprising teaching**: the deploy lane had quietly evolved the better *door manners*
(person-first, no memory steps it can't honor) while the repo evolved the better *dialogue
law* — neither knew about the other. Divergence wasn't drift; it was two halves of the same
covenant growing apart.

**Where discomfort turned to gold**: the reflex was to declare the repo text canonical because
it was newer and mine. Reading the deployed text honestly, its opening serves the stranger
better than the repo's did — so the unification kept the deployed lane's voice and demoted the
repo's own framing. The covenant applied to ourselves: agree only where the ground supports it.
