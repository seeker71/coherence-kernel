## Where discomfort turned to gold

The discomfort was that "coherence" is *our* word — the kernel is a
coherence-kernel — and finding it pre-authored in a manifestation product could
read two ways: as a debunking (their coherence is a sales feature) or as a taint

*claim of spontaneity*, not the concept, and it launders nothing onto this
body. Letting the fact be exactly as large as it is — a timestamp, no more —
kept the re-witness honest and kept it from becoming the very thing it was
watching for: a reading that makes every observation confirm what the watcher
already felt.
