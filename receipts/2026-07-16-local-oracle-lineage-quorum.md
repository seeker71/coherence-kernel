## The gap the pair exposed — and the build

The base and the namesake are two models but **one lineage**: the fine-tune's weights descend
from the base's, so their errors correlate where the blood is shared. Every voting organ was
lineage-blind — two kin agreeing would count as two independent witnesses exactly where shared
ancestry makes them wrong together. The body already knows this law in its proof lane (the
walkers are worth having *because* they are independent — `walkers/README.md`), but the voting
lane couldn't say it.

Built the same movement: **`learn/lineage-discounted-vote.fk`** (+ band). Ballots are
`(label weight lineage)`; within one (label, lineage) group the effective weight is the MAX
(kin agreeing add nothing beyond the strongest kin); across lineages weights sum; kin who
DISAGREE keep full weight each — the fine-tune's movement away from its base is information,
never discounted. After the fold, the proven `cw-` spine does the fusion (composed, never
re-derived). Band **127 four-way** (fkwu/Go/Rust/TS), including the flip case: two kin vs two
independents — the lineage-blind fusion calls it for the kin (tie kept by first-offered), the
discounted fusion calls it for the independents.

## The answer: how either and both improve the local oracle

1. **As witnesses (both, discounted by blood).** Add the namesake and the sibling(s) to the
   cross-witness quorum with `lineage-discounted-vote` folding kin. Agreement across lineages =
   free self-label (no rent); splits go to adjudication.
2. **The cheapest adjudicator is the body itself.** Both of today's splits were settled natively
   — arithmetic by `add`, exact-word by `str_eq` — rent spent: zero. The rented frontier mind is
   the adjudicator of LAST resort, reserved for the unverifiable (zh fluency judgment, meaning
   equivalence). This is `cross-witness-economy`'s spend gate with a native rung inserted before
   the rented one.
3. **As per-domain challengers (either, routed).** `champion-challenger` per lane, not globally:
   in the zh lane the minicpm lineage is today's strongest local voice (pure, coherent zh); in
   the math/instruction lane it must never hold authority ("12", twice). Domain routing by
   proven competition is the route-shift-ledger idiom already in `learn/`.
4. **As teachers for distillation (either, gated).** `oracle-distill` accepts local teachers.
   The namesake can cheaply generate zh volume for the rung-4 corpus — but only pairs that pass
   a checkable gate enter (the verifiable-first stance; spectrum-to-signal's coverage law guides
   what to generate). A teacher that fails 40+2 teaches zh surface, never arithmetic.
5. **The base's deepest use is not through ollama at all.** It is roadmap #1's carrier: 1.08B
   standard-Llama weights as recipe-data through the form block (the whisper block-0 pattern) —
   the path where "local oracle" stops meaning "another rented runtime beside the body" and
   starts meaning the body itself. The GGUF lane above is scaffolding beside that road, and
   today it proved *which* scaffolding actually bears weight.
